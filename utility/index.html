Government Guidelines & Policies

Know your rights and legal protections

Women's Safety Laws

Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This Act provides protection to women from domestic violence. It covers physical, sexual, verbal, emotional, and economic abuse. Women can file complaints and seek protection orders, residence orders, monetary relief, and custody orders.

  • Covers all forms of domestic violence
  • Protection orders available
  • Monetary compensation provisions
  • Residence rights protection

Sexual Harassment at Workplace Act, 2013

Mandatory formation of Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) in organizations with 10+ employees. Provides mechanism for complaint, inquiry, and redressal of sexual harassment at workplace.

  • ICC formation mandatory
  • Confidential complaint process
  • Time-bound inquiry (90 days)
  • Protection against retaliation

Criminal Law Amendment Act, 2013

Strengthened laws against sexual offenses including rape, acid attacks, stalking, and voyeurism. Enhanced punishment and faster trial procedures.

  • Expanded definition of sexual offenses
  • Mandatory minimum sentences
  • Fast-track courts for speedy trials
  • Victim compensation provisions

Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

Prohibits giving, taking, or demanding dowry. Both giving and receiving dowry is punishable with imprisonment and fine.

  • Complete prohibition of dowry
  • Punishment for all parties involved
  • Special courts for dowry cases
  • Protection for complainants

Immoral Traffic Prevention Act, 1956

Prevents trafficking of women and girls for commercial sexual exploitation. Provides for rescue, rehabilitation, and reintegration of victims.

  • Anti-trafficking provisions
  • Victim protection and rehabilitation
  • Punishment for traffickers
  • Special courts and procedures

Indecent Representation of Women Act, 1986

Prohibits indecent representation of women through advertisements, publications, writings, paintings, figures, or in any other manner.

Pre-Conception and Pre-Natal Diagnostic Techniques Act, 1994

Prohibits sex determination tests and female foeticide. Regulates genetic counseling centers and laboratories.

Commission of Sati Prevention Act, 1987

Prohibits the practice of Sati and glorification of Sati. Provides for punishment of those who abet, glorify or attempt to commit Sati.

Child Marriage Restraint Act, 1929

Prohibits child marriage and provides for punishment of those who perform, permit or promote child marriages.

Information Technology Act, 2000 (Cyber Crimes)

Addresses cyber crimes against women including cyber stalking, morphing, and online harassment. Provides legal framework for digital evidence.

Women's Health Policies

National Health Policy, 2017

Focuses on reproductive, maternal, newborn, child and adolescent health. Aims to reduce maternal mortality ratio to 100 per 100,000 live births by 2020.

  • Free healthcare services for women
  • Maternal mortality reduction targets
  • Reproductive health focus
  • Adolescent health programs

Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

Safe motherhood intervention providing cash assistance to pregnant women for institutional delivery. Reduces maternal and neonatal mortality.

  • Cash incentives for institutional delivery
  • Free delivery services
  • Post-delivery care
  • ASHA worker support

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Maternity benefit program providing ₹5,000 cash incentive to pregnant and lactating mothers for first living child.

  • ₹5,000 cash benefit
  • Nutrition support during pregnancy
  • Wage compensation
  • Health and nutrition education

Mission Indradhanush

Immunization program targeting pregnant women and children. Focuses on full immunization coverage in high-priority districts.

  • Tetanus vaccination for pregnant women
  • Child immunization coverage
  • Focus on high-priority areas
  • Mobile immunization units

Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK)

Adolescent health program addressing nutritional needs, mental health, and reproductive health of adolescents aged 10-19 years.

  • Adolescent-friendly health services
  • Menstrual hygiene management
  • Nutrition and anemia prevention
  • Mental health support

National Family Planning Program

Provides access to contraceptive services, family planning counseling, and reproductive health services.

LaQshya - Labour Room Quality Improvement Initiative

Improves quality of care in labour rooms and maternity operation theatres to reduce maternal and newborn mortality.

Surakshit Matritva Aashwasan (SUMAN)

Assures dignified, respectful and quality healthcare at no cost and zero tolerance for denial of services.

Ayushman Bharat - Health and Wellness Centres

Provides comprehensive primary healthcare including reproductive and child health services closer to communities.

National Iron Plus Initiative

Addresses iron deficiency anemia among children, adolescents, and women of reproductive age through supplementation.
